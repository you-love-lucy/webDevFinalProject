Inevitable

A Web Game

By Frances Jones

The internet.

A place of kittens, memes, and... murder.

You're an everyday person with a passion for true crime. This brings you to SolveIt, a website for other online detectives to try to crack cold cases.

It's just a causal hobby, until one day you notice something a bit odd.

Try to stop the Inevitable.

References:

Inspired by a multitude of sources, including 'Emily is Away', various learn-to-code murder mystery games, Criminal Minds, the 'Searching' and 'Missing' movies, and my own weird obsession with murder shows. Obviously, I do not condone real-world violence. Enjoy!
